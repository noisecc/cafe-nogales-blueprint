# 2. Brand Voice and Messaging

## Brand Voice Framework
- Informed
- Human
- Direct
- Respectful
(Explain each briefly and how it shows up in copy.)

## Voice Dos and Donts
**Do:**
- Name producer, process, elevation.
- Be transparent about quantities and availability.
- Keep sentences short.

**Don’t:**
- Use vague “premium/exotic/best” claims.
- Over-romanticize origin.
- Center the brand over the people behind the coffee.

## Sample Messaging per Channel
### Instagram (Launch)
Short, visual, concrete.  
Example: “New Peru lot from Cajamarca. 8 bags. Lime zest, jasmine, black tea. In catalog now.”

### Producer Stories
Warm, human, contextual.  
Example: “We’ve been cupping with Daniel for 3 seasons. This is his first Pink Bourbon.”

### Website / Catalog
Structured, confident, traceable.

## Brand Language Guide
- Words to use: clean, layered, focused, cupped, producer, community lot, slow-dried.
- Words to avoid: premium, exotic, perfect cup, humble farmer.
- Words to explain: microlot, experimental, sustainable (always add context).
